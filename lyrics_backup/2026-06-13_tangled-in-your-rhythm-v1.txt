[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when your shadow falls on mine
Tangled in your rhythm, losing track of time
Every breath you take is calling out my soul
Pulling me closer till I lose control

[Verse 1]
The evening sun is melting on the windowpane
A gentle breeze is bringing back the midnight rain
I see the longing hidden in your heavy eyes
A silent promise blooming under starlit skies

[Pre-Chorus]
And when you move, the whole room fades away
There is a secret that your quiet touches say
I want to memorize the curve of your embrace
And get lost forever in this lovely space

[Chorus]
Oh, I am tangled in your rhythm, wild and deep
A fiery fever that I always want to keep
You pull me under with a soft and burning sigh
We are dancing in the shadows, you and I

[Post-Chorus]
Tangled in your rhythm, holding onto you
Never gonna let you go, you know my heart is true

[Verse 2]
A brush of fingers sends a shiver down my spine
I taste the magic every time your lips meet mine
The embers glowing in the corner of the room
Are nothing to the way you chase away the gloom

[Pre-Chorus 2]
And when you move, the whole room fades away
There is a secret that your quiet touches say
I want to memorize the curve of your embrace
And get lost forever in this lovely space

[Chorus]
Oh, I am tangled in your rhythm, wild and deep
A fiery fever that I always want to keep
You pull me under with a soft and burning sigh
We are dancing in the shadows, you and I

[Post-Chorus 2]
Tangled in your rhythm, holding onto you
Never gonna let you go, you know my heart is true

[Bridge]
Tangled in your rhythm, where the shadows meet
Saeed is falling deeper with every single beat
Lost inside the magic of a sudden glance
Swept away completely by this endless dance
Nothing else matters when the night is closing in
Tracing the fire that is burning on the skin

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (Just close your eyes and let the feeling take over)
[Whispered] (There is nowhere else in the world I would rather be)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, I am tangled in your rhythm, wild and deep
A fiery fever that I always want to keep
You pull me under with a soft and burning sigh
We are dancing in the shadows, you and I
